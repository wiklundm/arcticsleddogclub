About us

  • Arctic sleddog club is situated in Abisko, above the arctic circle.

  • The clubs purpose is to spread the knowledge about the sleddog culture.

  • To educate, train for and participate in sleddog races.

  • To engage people in outdoor activities in an arctic environment.

  • To promote good dogcare and work against doping and discrimination in sports.

  • The club is part of (Swedish sleddog federation) and therby member at (Swedish national athletes federation).

  • Arctic sleddog club are following the regulations set by ESDRA and ISDVMA with additional Swedish restrictions.
